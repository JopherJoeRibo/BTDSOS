GUIDELINES

PWD(PERSON WITH DISABILITY) -
20% Discount

Present PWD ID upon ticket purchase.
No ID, no discount

Discount is applicable to local
and foreign PWDs

Exception: PWD ID is not required when
disablity is apparent i.e passenger
is an amputee

Senior Citizens -
20% Discount

Present Senior Citizen ID,
passport or other document proving
that holder is at least sixty (60)
years of age.

Student - 20% Discount

Discount can be valid availed throughout
the year including summer breaks, legal, and
special legal holidays.

Present valid student ID or school
registration card with student name,
photo and name of school
NO ID, NO DISCOUNT

Not eligible for discount are students in
medicine proper, law, graduate course
and short-term courses

Policy on Transportation of
Pet Animals

Victory Liner welcomes small to medium
breed cats and dogs to be transported in
its busses as long as they comply with the
guidelines set by the LTFRB Memorandum Circular
2020-003 or the "Supplemental Guidlines Allowing
Public Utility vehicles."
