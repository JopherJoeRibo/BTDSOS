About

Dagupan Bus Co., Inc., is a provincial bus company based in Dagupan, Pangasinan.

This company was a sister company of Saulog Transit up until the company's management was transferred to JAC Liner Inc., and Genesis Transport (as North Genesis) following Dagupan Bus' soldout in 2015. Dagupan Bus Co., Inc. started its operation in 1974 when they acquired the franchise of Villarey Transit, also a provincial bus company with only six Mitsubishi Fuso buses, through Saulog Transit Inc. to be able to expand its operation to Northern Luzon. They used an old livery of dirty white with maroon, red, and green stripes. Later, it has adopted the use of dirty white livery and red stripes during its re-fleeting of units in 2007.

FIVE STAR

Throughout the 30 years of commitment to serve, Five Star Bus has become one of the country’s top provincial operators. SOLID NORTH

Solid North Bus Pangasinan Solid North Transit, Inc. simply known as the Solid North bus. Also Known for its reliability and punctuality. The company was formed in 2010, as a subsidiary of Jac Liner Inc. Now they have 140+ buses to depart across destinations. It has regular trips from Cubao to San Carlos City, Dagupan, Bolinao, PITX to Baguio, Cabanatuan to Baguio, Bayambang to Baguio, and Dagupan to Baguio.
